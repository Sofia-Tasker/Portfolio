Incentive Engagement

Incentive Engagement is a microservice that implements a gamification system for the Incentive app. It will allow users earn points for tasks such as completing challenges, reading articles, or linking bank accounts. Users can then redeem points to earn prizes like gift cards, and will be able to compete with others in the global leaderboards. This microservice further encourages users to engage with the app through daily streaks and quizzes that only appear for a limited amount of time.
